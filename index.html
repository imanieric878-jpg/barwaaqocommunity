Who We Are

Nurturing Leadership, Shaping Futures

Barwaaqo Girls Initiative is a grassroots Community Based Organization based in Garissa County, Kenya.

We were founded to address the challenges faced by girls, including lack of access to education, menstrual hygiene products, and mentorship opportunities.

We work with communities to create sustainable solutions that uplift girls and young women.

Community outreach session at Tetu Primary School, Garissa County

Safe Learning Spaces

We establish safe, supportive environments where girls can ask questions, study, and thrive.

Peer Mentorship & Counseling

Girls connect with successful professional mentors who guide them in education and career options.

Empowerment through opportunities

To empower girls and young women through access to education support, menstrual health resources, mentorship, and life skills development, while advocating for gender equality, dignity, and sustainable community growth.

A world of thriving, self-reliant young leaders

To create a society where every girl is empowered, educated and protected with equal opportunities to achieve her full potential and lead positive change in her community.

What drives our work every day

  • Empathy & Care: Understanding and addressing the challenges faced by girls with compassion.
  • Integrity: Honoring our community obligations and maintaining transparency.
  • Inclusivity: Ensuring no girl is left behind due to social, cultural, or physical barriers.
  • Agency: Nurturing independence, confidence, and voice in every young woman.
What We Do

Our Impact Areas

We run tailored programs addressing specific educational, social, and professional needs of girls.

Academic & Scholarship Support

Facilitating school enrollments, providing study materials, and running localized tuition camps to make sure high school girls maintain excellent academic performance.

Mentorship & Career Circles

Connecting young girls directly with university students and professional career women for guidance, advice, life skills growth, and navigating professional entry.

Menstrual Health & Dignity Support

Providing free sanitary products, breaking period stigma through school workshops, and ensuring no girl misses school due to her menstrual cycle — restoring dignity and keeping girls in class.

Wellness & Reproductive Health Advocacy
